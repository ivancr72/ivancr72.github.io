wait, what the fuck is peejosa

peejosa is a conpidgin that formed in the r/conlangscirclejerk discord (which is not really related to the sub anymore but uhh). kind of like viossa, if you know what that is. basically, we're not allowed to translate from or to peejosa, it must be learned by immersion. people took words from the languages they knew, so it's just a mishmash of whatever other languages people happened to be speaking at the time. if you are able to pick up some words here, congratulations! you are learning peejosa.
